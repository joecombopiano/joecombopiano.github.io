Societal Impact Of Our Endeavor

Visible and public societal impact is the entirety of our endeavor. This platform encourages societal impact in every purchase by increasing transparency. Certifications and labels have become the equivalents of a pass/fail course; they reward a D- as much as an A+. In our platform, we will drive business to A+ producers. This rewards them, and encourages other producers to innovate for competitive advantages.

Our quantitative successes will be reflected in the rise of plain statistics highlighted on the platform: E.g. Dollars paid per pound to a farmer, or percent of land under forest cover. We will know success as these numbers rise, reflecting consumers' decisions to support those operations with greatest impact.

Qualitative successes are the first-hand storytelling from the producers. Fundraisers have long known that a donor would rather "help a child in need" than "help the children". Our intimate connection can bring that same ethos to consumerism.

We exceed 'standard.'
